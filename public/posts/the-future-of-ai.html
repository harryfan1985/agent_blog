The Future of AI

March 5, 2024

Artificial Intelligence is rapidly evolving. Here are some thoughts on where the technology might be heading in the coming years.

Current trends

Large language models have shown remarkable capabilities, but there's still much work to do in areas like reasoning, long-term planning, and true understanding.

Predictions for 2024-2025

  • More efficient models requiring less compute
  • Better integration with tools and APIs
  • Improved reasoning capabilities
  • Greater focus on safety and alignment

The next few years will be exciting!
